Assignment name  : ft_print_reverse_alphabet
Expected files   : ft_print_reverse_alphabet.c
Allowed functions: write
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write a program that displays the alphabet in lowercase, in descending order,
starting from the letter 'z', followed by a newline.

Example:

$> ./ft_print_reverse_alphabet | cat -e
zyxwvutsrqponmlkjihgfedcba$
