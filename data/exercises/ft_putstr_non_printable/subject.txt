Assignment name  : ft_putstr_non_printable
Expected files   : ft_putstr_non_printable.c
Allowed functions: write
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Create a function that displays a string. Non-printable characters must
be shown as a backslash followed by their two-digit lowercase
hexadecimal value (e.g. a newline becomes \\0a).

Your function must be declared as follows:

void	ft_putstr_non_printable(char *str);
