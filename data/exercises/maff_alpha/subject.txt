Assignment name  : maff_alpha
Expected files   : maff_alpha.c
Allowed functions: write
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write a program that displays the alphabet, with every second letter being
capitalized, in ascending order, starting with a lowercase 'a', followed by
a newline.

Example:

$> ./maff_alpha | cat -e
aBcDeFgHiJkLmNoPqRsTuVwXyZ$
