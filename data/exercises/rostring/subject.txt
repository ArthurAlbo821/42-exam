Assignment name  : rostring
Expected files   : rostring.c
Allowed functions: write, malloc, free
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write a program that takes a string and displays this string with its words
rotated by one to the left, followed by a newline.

This means the first word takes the place of the last one, and every other
word is shifted one place to the left.

A word is a section of string delimited by spaces/tabs, or by the start/end
of the string. The displayed words must be separated by exactly one space.

If the number of arguments is not 1, the program displays a newline.

Examples:

$> ./rostring "abc   " | cat -e
abc$
$> ./rostring "Que la      lumiere soit et la lumiere fut" | cat -e
la lumiere soit et la lumiere fut Que$
$> ./rostring "     AkjhZ zLKIJz , 23y" | cat -e
zLKIJz , 23y AkjhZ$
$> ./rostring | cat -e
$
